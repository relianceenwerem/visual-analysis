Pattern 01
The response gap
Every image shows a mismatch between scale of destruction and capacity to respond. Tracking the arc from image 01 to 05 shows the same axis of harm meeting radically different institutional contexts.
Pattern 02
Vertical witness
A surviving upright — steeple, tower skeleton, funnel itself — anchors each frame. The vertical reads as ironic testimony, still standing while everything around it falls or is submerged.
Pattern 03
Human as scale
People function less as protagonists than as instruments — making the depth of water, the height of debris, the enormity of rubble visible. Only image 03 dispenses with this entirely.
